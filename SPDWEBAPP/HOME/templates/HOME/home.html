Sigma Phi Delta: Beta-Omicron Chapter

University of Missouri, Columbia

The Beta-Omicron Chapter of Sigma Phi Delta is the premiere social and professional Engineering fraternity at the University of Missouri. Our closely-knit brotherhood is committed to developing strong professional skills and relationships for their future careers, encouraging positive social skills to aid in their personal endeavors, fostering scholastic success in their academic pursuits, and promoting the development of life-long friendships.


Keep up with our events on Instagram!

One in three Sigma Phi Delta members participate in co-ops, internships, and study abroad opportunities. Sigma Phi Delta is dedicated to helping their brothers to secure similar opportunities both during and after their college experience. Sigma Phi Delta also regularly hosts professional speakers, resume reviews, and other professional development events for their members.

Sigma Phi Delta recognizes the importance of giving back to their community. They are dedicated to volunteering their time at local non-profit organizations and benefiting their national philanthropy FIRST©. Some of their frequent contributions include The Food Bank for Central & Northeast Missouri, Ronald McDonald House, RAMS, and Adopt-A-Spot.

Their national philanthropy FIRST© engages K-12 students in exciting, mentor-based, research and robotics programs that help them become science and technology leaders, as well as well-rounded contributors to society. FIRST© aims to empower students to enrich their lives, realize their potential, and become outstanding future employees and citizens by fostering a passion for STEM subjects.

Success
Sigma Phi Delta prides itself on their consistent academic success. Each semester, their chapter GPA is well above the all-male engineering average, and they consistently rank well against other fraternity houses on campus.
Assistance
In order to ensure all of their active members and new members are held to the same standard, each member is assigned an academic mentor in the same degree program. They are also required to attend weekly study tables where they are surrounded by an abundance of knowledge and resources in order to help them succeed.
Opportunity
In the interest of giving their members as many opportunities as possible for them to further their academic and professional careers, they frequently have professors, industry professionals, and employers present at their weekly chapter meetings.

You truly aren't experiencing college if your only friends are your textbooks. Sigma Phi Delta members are anything but the "typical" engineer. While they take the same classes, help each other out with homework, and study together, they also know how to have a good time after the hard work is finished! Sigma Phi Delta members regularly participate in formals, tailgates, socials, float trips, cook outs, and intramural sports.
